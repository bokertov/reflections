Q: How did viewing a diff between two versions of a file help you see the bug
that was introduced?

A: Diff tool helps us skip the common parts of two files and concentrate on
the difference.

Q: How could having easy access to the entire history of a file make you a more
efficient programmer in the long term?

A: We don't need to worry about maintaining different versions or losing copies
. We can concentrate more on the logic itself rather than such logistics.
